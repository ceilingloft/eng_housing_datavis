House prices in England, 1999-2019

House prices in England have risen steeply over the past 20 years, whereas average wages have remained stagnant. Here you can explore how median house prices and median earnings have changed over time across England.

The data displayed by the map is the ratio of median house price to earnings.

What does the ratio represent?
The ratio is median house price (£) to median earnings (£). So a ratio of 4 means the median house price is 4x median yearly earnings In other words, if represents the average house price in England, and represents the average yearly earnings, then a ratio of 4 would be represented by:
